# Consolidated reading list

A single-page index of the course readings, grouped by theme. The definitive, week-by-week list
lives in [`syllabus.md`](../syllabus.md); this is a convenience view. Because AI moves quickly,
the 2025–2026 items — especially the comparative-governance cases — should be refreshed each time
the course is taught.

## Books

- *Recoding America* — Jennifer Pahlka (2023) — **start here** for the public-sector lens
- *Co-Intelligence* — Ethan Mollick (2024)
- *The Alignment Problem* — Brian Christian (2020)
- *Power and Prediction* — Agrawal, Gans & Goldfarb (2022)
- *A Civic Technologist's Practice Guide* — Cyd Harrell (2020)
- *Empire of AI* — Karen Hao (2025)
