# Formats

Condensed and audience-adapted versions of the *AI in the Public Sector* course. The full
syllabus lives at [`../syllabus.md`](../syllabus.md); these are alternative packagings of the
same material for different audiences and time constraints.

- **[`political-staffers-intensive.md`](./political-staffers-intensive.md)** — a 3-half-day
  intensive (flexes to 2–3 full days) for ministerial, legislative, and campaign staff. Reframed
  around three staffer questions: *can I trust this?*, *can I use it without getting burned?*, and
  *how do I advise my principal?*

> ⚠️ **Localize before delivering.** Any format that touches confidentiality, records and
> access-to-information, or approved AI tools relies on rules that vary by jurisdiction and office.
> Adapt that guidance to your own government's actual AI-use and records policy.
