# Assignment 2 — Briefing Note 2 (20%)

**Length:** ~2,500 words · **Format:** individual · **Due:** after Session 9

## The task

Write a briefing note on one or more topics from Sessions 4–9 — procurement and the machinery
of government, building and modernizing public-sector AI, data and algorithmic harm, the human
in the loop, or responsible AI. This note should go beyond description to **analysis** and
**concrete recommendations**.

Engage substantively with **at least two course readings**, and apply an explicit **tensions
lens**: name the central trade-off your topic forces on government — what is gained, what is
risked, and who bears the cost — and let that shape your recommendations.

## What to include

- A clear problem statement and why it matters for the public sector
- Substantive engagement with at least two course readings and, where useful, external sources
- An explicit treatment of the governing tension(s) at stake
- Realistic, actionable recommendations

## Using AI

As with Briefing Note 1: AI is permitted as a drafting aid, must be cited, apply the course
custom instructions, append an interaction log, and verify everything yourself.

## Graded on

- Quality of argument and analysis
- Engagement with course materials and external sources
- How well the central trade-off is surfaced and handled
- Realistic, actionable recommendations
