# Assignment 1 — Briefing Note 1 (10%)

**Length:** ~1,500 words · **Format:** individual · **Due:** after Session 3

## The task

Write a briefing note on a topic from Sessions 1–3 — AI basics, what modern AI can and cannot
do, or the limits of data. Your audience is a **Deputy Minister who is not technical**. Explain
the topic clearly, identify its key implications for the public sector, and offer preliminary
recommendations.

Write it the way a real briefing note reads: purpose up top, plain language, structured, and
decision-useful.

## What to include

- **Purpose / issue** — why this note, and why now
- **Key information / context** — the concept, explained for a non-technical senior reader
- **Implications for the public sector** — why a Deputy Minister should care
- **Preliminary recommendations** — sensible next steps, appropriately hedged
- **References**

## Using AI

You may use AI as a drafting and ideation aid. If you do, apply the
[course AI custom instructions](../instructions/student-research-custom-instructions.md),
cite your use, and append a short log of your key AI interactions (excluded from the word count).
Verify every fact and citation yourself.

## Graded on

- Clarity of explanation for a non-technical audience
- Technical accuracy
- Policy relevance and actionable framing
- Professional writing and structure
