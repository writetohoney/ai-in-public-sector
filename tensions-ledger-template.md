# The tensions ledger

A running log you keep through the course. Each week, capture the central trade-off that week's
theme forces on government. The discipline is simple: **name what is gained, what is risked, and
who bears the cost** — and resist the urge to resolve the tension. The point is to see it clearly.

Your ledger feeds directly into Briefing Note 2 and the final project.

## How to use it

- One row per session.
- Keep entries short — a few sentences per cell is plenty.
- "Who bears the cost?" is the most important column. Be specific about *which* people or groups.
- Note where AI *sharpens* the tension rather than creating it.
